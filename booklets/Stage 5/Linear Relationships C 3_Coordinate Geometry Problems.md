By Pythagoras\' theorem, ΔXYZ is right-angled.

$$m_{1} \times \ m_{3} = \frac{5}{7} \times \ \left( - \frac{7}{5} \right) = \  - 1$$

Lines 1 and 3 are perpendicular, so triangle is right-angled.
