# Line and Rotational Symmetry

## Line Symmetry

- A shape has **line symmetry** when folding it along a line creates two
  identical halves that match perfectly - each half is a **reflection**
  of the other.

## Rotational Symmetry

- **Rotational symmetry** exists when a shape looks identical after
  spinning through 360°.

- The **order** of rotational symmetry is how many times a shape matches
  its starting position during one full turn.

- All shapes have at least **order 1** rotational symmetry, since they
  match their starting position after one full turn.
