# Simple Cubic Equations

- **Cubic equations** have unknowns to the power of 3: $x^{3}$ "$x$
  cubed"

- **Cube rooting** $\sqrt[3]{}$ is the inverse operation of cubing

<!-- -->

- A cubic equation in the form $ax^{3} = k$ always has **one** real
  solution.

- Both positive and negative numbers can be cube rooted.

- To solve cubic equations:

1.  Isolate $x^{3}$

2.  Cube root both sides.
