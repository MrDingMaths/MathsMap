# Overview

## Syllabus Content

**MA5-ARE-C-01** solves problems involving the surface area of right
prisms and practical problems involving the area of composite shapes and
solids

**Develop and apply the formula for surface areas of cylinders**

- Recognise the curved surface of a cylinder as a rectangle and apply
  this knowledge to calculate the area of the curved surface

- Develop and apply the formula to find the surface area of a closed
  cylinder: $A = 2{\pi r}^{2} + 2\pi rh,$ where $r$ is the length of the
  radius and $h$ is the perpendicular height
