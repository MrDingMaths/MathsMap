# Angles of Elevation and Depression

- **Line of sight** is a straight line from your eye to an object.

- Angle of **elevation**: The angle you turn to look up from the
  horizontal.

- Angle of **depression**: The angle you turn to look down from the
  horizontal.

- The **horizontal** is a line parallel to the ground.

- To find angles, we can use these angle relationships:

  - Angles in a right-angle sum to $90{^\circ}$

  - Angle sum of a triangle is $180{^\circ}$

  - Alternate angles on parallel lines are equal.
