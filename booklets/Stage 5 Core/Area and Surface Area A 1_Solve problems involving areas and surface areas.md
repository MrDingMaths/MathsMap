# Overview

## Syllabus Content

**MA5-ARE-C-01** solves problems involving the surface area of right
prisms and practical problems involving the area of composite shapes and
solids

**Solve problems involving areas and surface areas**

- Solve practical problems involving the areas of composite shapes

- Identify the edge lengths and the faces making up the surface area of
  prisms

- Recognise and justify whether a diagram represents a net of a right
  prism

- Create and rearrange nets of right prisms

- Find the surface areas of prisms, given their nets, excluding curved
  surfaces

- Solve problems involving surface areas of prisms, excluding curved
  surfaces
