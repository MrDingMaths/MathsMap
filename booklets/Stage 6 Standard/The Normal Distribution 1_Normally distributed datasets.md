# The Normal Distribution

- Characteristics of a normal distribution:

  - Most of the data is located at the centre and decreases as you go
    toward the tails.

  - It is **symmetrical** about the mean

  - Has the **same mean, mode and median**.

  - Has a **bell-shape**.

- The graph of a normal distribution is called a **bell curve** due to
  its shape.

- The normal distribution is the most important distribution in
  statistics; given a large enough dataset, many naturally occurring
  characteristics have this bell curve (although not perfectly).
