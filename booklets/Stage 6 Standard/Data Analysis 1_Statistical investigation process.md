# Overview

## Syllabus Content

**MST-11-08** displays and analyses datasets using summary statistics
and graphical representations

**Statistical investigation process**

- Identify an issue and pose a question to a targeted population to
  gather statistical information

- Develop a survey by applying questionnaire design principles of clear
  language, unambiguous questions and consideration of number of choices

- Examine issues of privacy, bias, ethics and responsiveness to diverse
  groups and cultures
