# Overview

## Syllabus Content

**MA4-VOL-C-01** applies knowledge of volume and capacity to solve
problems involving right prisms and cylinders

**Develop and apply the formula to find the volume of a prism to solve
problems**

- Develop the formula for the volume of a prism:
  $V = base\ area \times height$, leading to $V = Ah$

- Apply the formula for the volume of a prism to prisms with uniform
  cross-sections to solve problems
